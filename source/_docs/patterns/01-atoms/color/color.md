# Color

These are the “go to” colors when designing or building communications for Western’s audiences.

## Main Colors
These should be the most prominent colors in a design, comprising about 90% of the visual space.

## Tints & Shades
In addition to the base colors, use of the following tints and shades is also acceptable. Tints and shades should be used in similar proportion to accent colors, comprising about 10% or less of the overall design.

<div className="swatch-wrapper">
  <div className="dark-blue accent gradient color-swatch">
    <h3>Shades of Dark Blue</h3>
    <p>Any amount of black added to the base dark blue.</p>
  </div>
  <div className="blue accent gradient color-swatch">
    <h3>Tints of Light Blue</h3>
    <p>Any amount of white added to the base blue. Watch out for text contrast!</p>
  </div>
</div>
<div className="swatch-wrapper">
  <div className="bw accent gradient color-swatch">
    <h3>Black, Gray, and White</h3>
    <p>Any shade or tint on the spectrum between the brand black and white, with the brand gray as an intermediate value</p>
  </div>
</div>
